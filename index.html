Approach

We defend our clients against all criminal allegations, at all trial and appeal courts including the Supreme Court of Armenia if needed. We travel across Armenia (and beyond) to represent our clients.

For over 5 years we have defended dozens of clients accused with criminal offences such as theft, assault, impaired driving (DUI), sexual assault, internet crimes, murder, fraud, uttering threats, drug possession and trafficking.

We believe in every citizen’s right to be presumed innocent, to have a fair trial and to be treated fairly by the police and the courts. We will work hard to obtain the best available remedy for you, be it acquittal, termination of the charges or a plea resolution involving less serious charges or a more lenient sentence. With us you will be helped by an experienced criminal lawyer in a professional, honest, confidential and affordable manner.
